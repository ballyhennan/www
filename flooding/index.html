Flooding at 9 Ballyhennan Crescent, Tarbet

This webpage has been created to provide all the information about the flooding events that take place at 9 Ballyhennan Crescent

A few things to note:

  • I get 6-10 bad flooding events like this every year. Most occur in autumn and winter.
  • I have sandbags down 365 days a year to divert flood water into my house drainage.
  • Sometimes the amount of flood water is so great it tops the sandbags that I use direct the water into my house drainage.
  • I also have sandbags down the side of my house to stop the really bad flooding getting under my house.
  • Even with the sandbags at the side of my house, the water has started to eat away at the stone of my house (more below).

SSE Investigation

The image below shows what the SSE land directly above my house currently looks like on a dry day (a hole of water reflecting the trees above).

Your colleague Neil had a look at the area and thought it could be a burst large field drain, which would explain the amount of water cascading into my garden.

Neil dug down, but in the time available, he couldn't find a pipe. We think it could be many feet down.

As you can see in the picture below, this area/hole is always full of water. Even after our very dry summer this year, the area had water in it.
